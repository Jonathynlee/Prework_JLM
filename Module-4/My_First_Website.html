Linkin Park

Bio

Linkin Park is a 90's band that continued to play music till 2017 when the lead singer, Chester Bennington, died. Their music spanned many genres as they paved the way for heavy metal and punk music. They had 9 major albums; Hybrid Theory, Reanimation, Meterora (This is proabably the most famous of their albums), Minutes to Midnight, A Thousand Suns, Living Things, Recharged, The Hunting Party and One More Light.
